GCodeIn

IRC

GCodeIn's IRC channel is #gcodein on chat.freenode.net.

You don't have to keep everything on-topic, just follow the rules.

Rules

  • No arguing or flaming
  • No trolling
  • No swearing
  • No spamming
  • Keep it age-appropriate for 13-17 year olds
  • No discussion of GCI prizes before the announcement on the Google Open Source blog

Students of particular orgs may register #gcodein-<org>; these rules apply in those channels too.
